Fried eggs with avocado and fresh flowers

We're always looking for new ways to enjoy our favorite foods, and this one is definitely a keeper

Prep time:5 minutes
Cook time:10 minutes
Serves:2

Ingredients:

2 eggs per person

1/2 avocado per person

1 handful of fresh flowers

Directions:

In a medium-sized pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium heat. Crack the eggs into the pan and cook for above 3 minutes on each side, or until they reach your preferred doneness. Remove from pan. Add 1 tablespoon of oil to the same pan and saute the avocado slices.
